Seed the day Toast

Recipe by: Hannah
Serves: 1
Time: 3 minutes

  • 1 slice of bread
  • 2 tbsp natural peanut butter
  • Chia seeds
  • Hemp seeds
  • Sunflower seeds
  • Pepitas
  • Honey(optional)
  1. Toast your bread until golden brown.
  2. Spread peanut butter on bread.
  3. Top with seeds and a drizzle of honey if desired.
